Rackz Gallery X Gerard Riera

Pandora

The lights go on! After more than two years of recording and work, the Rackz Gallery team shows the world its first piece of art in the form of a video. A 15 minutes long piece where the style and aesthetics of Skateboard prevails and where the most prominent and promising riders of the national scene transmit the philosophy and principles of what the team understands as Skateboard.

To commemorate this long awaited launch and immortalize the best moments of this journey, the company has counted with the collaboration of Gerard Riera, one of the best photographers in the world in the sector and who has a countless number of publications in magazines such as Trasher, FREE, SOLO or Dogway.

Gerard and the team share a very clear philosophy in this world and that is that Skateboarding is art and for that reason have joined forces to launch the first project of NFT's where both offer the possibility that, defenders of the same philosophy, be part of the project by acquiring these unique works of art.Many of the works have been published in magazines and now the team gives the possibility for people to be the sole owner of it. Anticipate the future and join the movement.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for 'non-fungible token. An NFT is basically data that is accounted for in a digital ledger, and that data represents something specific and unique. An NFT can, for example, represent a unique piece of art or a game token.

What does “mint” mean?

NFT Minting is the process by which your digital art or digital content becomes a part of the Ethereum blockchain.
